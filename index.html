How It Works

Simple, Safe & Beautiful Process

Our signature 3D casting is gentle, hygienic, and safe — even for babies from 1 week old, capturing every detail in just 15 minutes.

1

Gentle Impression

Quick 2-minute impression using warm, food-safe alginate. Feels like play-dough!

2

Precision Casting

Premium resin is poured to preserve every line, curve, and emotion.

3

Artful Finishing

Each piece is hand-refined, polished, painted, and mounted with care. Your timeless keepsake is ready in 10–15 days.
